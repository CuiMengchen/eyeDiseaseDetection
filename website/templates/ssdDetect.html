eElectronics

This Eye Disease Detection System is dedicated to rapid detection and classification of eye diseases, and can be applied to training, front-end pre-screening classification, self-health monitoring and other purposes. The user fills in the name and age according to the instructions, and uploads the fundus photo. After clicking the test, the system will predict and output the most likely disease, and finally introduce the relevant disease knowledge
